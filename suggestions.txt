================================================================================
           FINPULSE AI — VISUAL & CONTENT UPGRADE SUGGESTIONS FOR DAILY DIGEST
================================================================================

To transform the daily digest newsletter from a basic HTML layout into an
elegant, premium, and highly informative briefing, we should implement updates
across visual aesthetics, mobile responsiveness, and content density.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Visual & Layout Enhancements (Premium Design System)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Clean Typography:
  * Replace the default "Arial" with a curated sans-serif stack optimized for 
    high-readability on all screens (e.g., 'Inter', -apple-system, system-ui, 
    'Segoe UI', Roboto).
  
- Curated Color Palette:
  * Avoid raw primary colors. Use a sophisticated Slate, Navy, and Accent palette:
    - Primary Navy: #0F172A (Deep Slate/Navy for text/headers)
    - Background: #F8FAFC (Soft grey-white for email body)
    - Card Background: #FFFFFF (Pure white cards for high contrast)
    - Accents: #2563EB (Brand blue) or #10B981 (Subtle emerald green for gains)
    - Border Tint: #E2E8F0 (Super thin, light slate borders)

- Card-Based Structure:
  * Wrap each section (AI Summary, Watchlist, Top Headlines) in a white card container 
    with a soft border-radius (e.g., 10px) and subtle padding. This separates sections 
    visually and mimics premium publications like Bloomberg or Morning Brew.
  
- Sophisticated Unicode Markers:
  * Replace noisy emojis (🔥, 📌, 📈, 🏛️, 🌍) with elegant, minimalist accent characters:
    - ✦ (Sparkle/AI)
    - ↗ (Bullish/Upward mention)
    - ↳ (Sub-point or link)
    - • (Clean bullet)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. Content Reduction & Noise Filtering (Curation > Volume)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Right now, the newsletter contains up to 25 items (10 headlines + 5 market + 5 policy 
+ 5 global). We can drastically improve readability by focusing on high-signal content:

- AI Summary (Keep & Highlight):
  * Keep the "Groq AI Executive Market Briefing" right at the top. It serves as 
    the perfect elevator pitch of the day's events.
  
- Condensed Watchlist Grid:
  * Limit the "Securities to Watch" to the top 4-5 dynamic mentions.
  * Display them as horizontal inline badges or pills instead of a vertical list.
  
- Top Headlines (Reduce from 10 to 5):
  * Only show the top 5 highest-impact headlines (based on the absolute sentiment score).
  * This keeps the email short and highly engaging.

- Categorized Sections (Reduce from 5 to 3 per category):
  * Market & Stocks: Max 3 items.
  * Economy & Policy: Max 3 items.
  * Global & Industry: Max 3 items.
  * Skip duplicates or general navigation links (e.g., "Hello Login" boilerplate 
    scraped from the header of Moneycontrol).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
